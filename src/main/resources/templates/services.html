Our Process — Interior Design

A clear, collaborative journey from concept to completion — thoughtfully planned and expertly delivered.

1

Discovery & Consultation

We learn about your space, lifestyle, preferences and budget through site visits and initial discussions to capture your vision.

2

Concept & Space Planning

We create layout options, moodboards and theme proposals to explore the best use of your space and style direction.

3

Design Development & 3D Visualization

Detailed plans, material palettes and realistic 3D renders bring the concept to life so you can preview the final design.

4

Material Selection & Budget Finalization

We recommend finishes and furnishings, prepare transparent cost estimates, and fine-tune selections to match the budget.

5

Execution & Project Management

We manage contractors, supervise works onsite, and coordinate deliveries to ensure the project runs on time and to spec.

6

Final Styling & Handover

Styling, accessories and finishing touches are added. We perform quality checks and hand over the completed project.

7

Post-Project Support

We provide minor touch-ups and ongoing support to ensure your space remains beautiful and functional.
